Wimwa Homes

Your Home Away From Home

Out of town comfort homes

Wimwa homes is a prestigious and well designed 4 bedroom all ensuite townhouses that has only 5 units in the gated development. It is located along the Nyeri-Nanyuki highway, after Nanyuki airstrip, behind Delta Petrol Station, 400 metres from the highway.

All houses have individual titles on 300sq feet each

About Wimwa Homes

The Estate is well spaced and planned ensuring each town house has the privacy it deserves while at the same time ensuring communual living. Each town house has a spacious lounge with a dining area that is open to a large kitchen that is perfect for family meal preparation. The kitchen also has a spacious pantry.

Upstairs there are 3 large and well spaced bedrooms with large windows to let in ample lighting and having scenic view of the surroundings. All 4 bedrooms are ensuite having in-built wardrobes. The town houses also have a large master bedroom and is spacious, has enough closet space for his and hers and a large master bathroom.

Each town house comes with a detached servants quaters and a laundry room including a washing area with provision for a washing machine.

Each unit goes for Ksh. 20 million (neg)
